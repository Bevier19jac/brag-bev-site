Brag & Bev L.L.C.

Innovation-first sanitation systems for real-world use.

Headquartered in Kalamazoo, Michigan, Brag & Bev L.L.C. bridges manual cleaning and professional sanitation outcomes through practical, patent-pending technology.

Mission: To revolutionize waste management by offering convenient, sustainable, and affordable cleaning solutions for households, businesses, and municipalities.

Sustainability

Design choices aim to reduce avoidable waste, excessive rework, and unnecessary cleanup cycles.

User-Centric Engineering

One-handed handling, controlled activation, and practical field use remain key engineering constraints.

Environmental Responsibility

Development decisions prioritize responsible deployment and realistic operating conditions.
