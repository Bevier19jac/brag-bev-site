Dumpster Diver

Purpose-built sanitation for outdoor bins.

Dumpster Diver is a patent-pending, disposable, eco-friendly cleaning innovation designed to simplify trash bin sanitation. The current program is focused on prototype finalization, not mass production.

Activation

Prototype architecture includes manual safety priming through an integrated pull tab. Inertial and impact-based activation concepts are under evaluation.

Coverage & Foam

High-expansion foam pathways are intended to adhere to interior walls and help dissolve grime and neutralize odors. Dispersion behavior is currently being refined.

Control

Self-righting architecture through a weighted base is designed to improve orientation control and repeatable interior coverage.

Core Priorities

Reliable Activation

Improving controlled activation behavior while maintaining practical handling and safety intent.

Effective Spray Dispersion

Tuning foam expansion and dispersion characteristics for more dependable interior performance.

Full Interior Coverage

Designing for consistent wall contact and improved cleaning reach inside outdoor bins.
