No Splash No Streak

Drop. Bloom. Go.

Concept Under Feasibility Review

No Splash No Streak is an exploratory pre-use bathroom hygiene system designed around a compact dry disk that is dropped directly into toilet water before use. Upon contact with water, the disk is intended to bloom into a temporary floating surface layer that may help reduce splash-back, reduce bowl streaking, and release a light fragrance barrier.

This concept is being evaluated as a potential future Brag & Bev product platform alongside Dumpster Diver. While Dumpster Diver addresses outdoor bin sanitation, No Splash No Streak explores indoor bathroom hygiene through the same product-development philosophy: practical design, simple user experience, staged technical validation, and responsible claim control.

Product formats under exploration

Home Dispenser

A countertop dispenser concept designed to keep dry disks moisture-protected and ready for everyday bathroom use.

Travel Singles

Individually wrapped disks designed for discreet use in hotels, offices, travel, shared bathrooms, and other high-stress restroom situations.

Commercial Dispenser

A locked wall-mounted dispenser concept for hospitality, offices, medical facilities, events, and premium public restroom environments.

Development priorities

  • Bloom speed and consistency
  • Float behavior and target-zone stability
  • Splash and streak reduction evaluation under controlled methods (outcomes not assumed)
  • Fragrance release and residue review
  • Moisture stability in bathroom environments
  • If pursued: material dispersal and plumbing-pathway evaluation under protocol-defined conditions (not stated as current performance)
  • Consumer acceptance and use-behavior validation

Disclaimer

No Splash No Streak is an exploratory product concept under feasibility review. Communications about environmental profile, plumbing-pathway behavior after use, dispersal, or performance require validation before commercial launch or consumer marketing.
